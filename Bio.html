Pancho's Bio

Originally from Cholula, Puebla, I was raised in Los Angeles, California. I was taught Spanish with a belt and bible.

I am a bi-cultural, highly motivated individual, self taught in a range of computer skills. My passions in life are Security, Networking, Linux, Programming, web-development, Complex Systems and AI.

I discovered my love for creative problem solving during my time at Intel resolving arduous technical issues and was thrilled to be able to apply that to programming.

As a software Developer I feel comfortable with React, VueJS, jquery, nodejs/express, neo4j, mongoDB, SQL, kafka, Github workflows, Kubernetes and Docker. I love collaborating and helping others in order to build beautiful software.
