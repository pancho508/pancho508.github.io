Extra - Why programming?

I did not have much interest to program during my youth.

However, last year while surfing the internet I read Complexity: A Guided Tour by Melanie Mitchell by coincidence I was also playing a game called Nier Automata.

I had done a few tinkering projects on my pc with AI but nothing solid nor concrete. However, after finishing both the book and the game. There was a fire in me to learn everything I could about this beautiful unknown that is complexity.

In reaching that goal I am prepared to sacrifice everything. HolaCode is helping me take that first step.
